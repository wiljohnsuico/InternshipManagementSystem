Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Job Listings page! Here's a quick guide to help you:

  • Searching for Jobs: Use the search bar to find specific companies or roles. Filter by location and compensation type (paid/unpaid).
  • Applying for Jobs: Click the "Apply" button on any job listing. Make sure to prepare your resume and cover letter.
  • Application Status: Once you've applied, the button will change to "Applied" and the job card will be highlighted.
  • Track Applications: Visit "My Applications" page to track the status of your applications.
