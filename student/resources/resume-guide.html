Resume Writing Guide

Essential Resume Components

  • Contact Information

    Include your full name, email, phone number, and location

  • Professional Summary

    Brief overview of your key qualifications and career goals

  • Education

    List your academic qualifications, GPA (if above 3.0), relevant coursework

  • Skills

    Technical skills, soft skills, and any certifications

  • Experience

    Work experience, internships, projects, and volunteer work

Tips for Success

Keep it Concise

Limit your resume to 1-2 pages maximum

Tailor Your Content

Customize your resume for each position

Use Action Words

Start bullet points with strong action verbs

Keywords Matter

Include relevant industry keywords

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Using generic objectives

Including personal information (age, marital status)

Spelling and grammar errors

Using an unprofessional email address
