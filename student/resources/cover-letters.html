Cover Letter Writing Guide

Cover Letter Structure

Header

  • Your contact information
  • Date
  • Recipient's information

Opening Paragraph

  • State the position you're applying for
  • Mention how you learned about it
  • Express enthusiasm for the role

Body Paragraphs

  • Highlight relevant experiences
  • Connect skills to job requirements
  • Provide specific examples

Closing Paragraph

  • Reiterate interest in position
  • Thank the reader
  • Include call to action

Writing Tips

Be Original

Customize each letter for the specific position and company

Keep it Concise

Limit to one page with 3-4 paragraphs

Research

Show knowledge of company's values and culture

Proofread

Check grammar, spelling, and formatting

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Using a generic template without customization

Repeating your entire resume

Making it too long or too short

Forgetting to update company details
