Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for an internship?

To apply for an internship: 1) Update your resume, 2) Browse available positions in the Job Listings page, 3) Click the "Apply" button on the position you're interested in, 4) Fill out the application form and submit your resume and cover letter.

What should I include in my resume?

Your resume should include: personal information, educational background, relevant skills, projects, any work experience, and extracurricular activities. Make sure to highlight achievements and skills relevant to the internship you're applying for.

How can I track my application status?

Visit the "My Applications" page to view all your submitted applications. Each application will show its current status (Pending, Under Review, Accepted, or Rejected). You'll receive email notifications when there are updates to your applications.

Can I apply for multiple internships?

Yes, you can apply for multiple internships simultaneously. However, make sure to tailor your resume and cover letter for each position. Keep track of your applications through the My Applications page.
